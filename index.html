PROJECT BRIEF / V0.1

Radio archaeology,
without pretending.

Ghost Signals is a local portfolio prototype for exploring public, receive-only radio sources. The interface can generate a test feed or analyze audio you explicitly share from an open receiver tab.

Public WebSDR receivers remain in their own interfaces, honoring their operators and terms. This prototype does not transmit, bypass access controls, or decode encrypted communications.
